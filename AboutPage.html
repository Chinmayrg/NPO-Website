What exactly do we do to fight climate change?

There's four main activities we partake in to combat climate change.
Our main course of action is planting trees. Trees consume Carbon Dioxide in the air to grow.
Another action we might take is consulting with large manufacturing companies on how to reduce their carbon emissions.
We also protest the companies that cut down millions of trees everyday. The final action we take
is, of course, educating the general public on the dangers of climate change, and how they can join the battle.

Does planting trees really help?

Yes! The impact of planting one tree alone might seem miniscule,
but what if we planted millions? Thats our objective. Last year alone
we planted 56 million trees. Trees also provide numerous side benefits
besides consuming CO2 and releasing O2, such as improving mental health,
reducing the ambient temperature of neighbourhoods, and providing homes to myriad animals.
For more information, watch this video by the Infographics show.

What can I do to help?

Great question. We'd advise that you go out there and plant a tree or two
in your yard and watch over its growth. If you don't like the sound of doing it
yourself, then donate to us and have our team of trained professionals plant
trees for you in areas around the world that need them. Don't have any money
to spare? No problem! Just spread the news to friends and family.
Remember: Together we can save our planet!
